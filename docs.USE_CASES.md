# 🌾 Use Cases — HaritBandhu

## 🌱 1. Soil Health Analysis (Before Farming)
- Farmer enters soil parameters (NPK, pH, organic carbon)
- System analyzes soil health
- Suggests suitable crops and fertilizers
- Helps in improving yield and soil quality

---

## 🌤️ 2. Weather Alerts & Smart Decisions
- System continuously monitors weather conditions
- Provides alerts for:
  - Rainfall
  - Temperature changes
  - Extreme conditions (storm, heatwave)
- Helps farmers take preventive actions at every stage

---

## 🌿 3. Crop Advisory & Selection
- Farmer selects season (Kharif/Rabi)
- System suggests best crops based on:
  - Soil health
  - Weather conditions
- Provides complete farming guidance

---

## 🔬 4. Pest Detection & Treatment
- Farmer uploads crop image
- AI detects pest/disease
- System provides treatment suggestions instantly

---

## 🤖 5. AI Chat Assistant
- Farmers ask questions in Hindi/English
- System provides real-time guidance using AI
- Covers farming, schemes, pest issues, etc.

---

## 🏛️ 6. Government Schemes Awareness
- Farmers explore schemes based on category
- Get eligibility and application links
- Improves access to subsidies and benefits

---

## 💰 7. Market Prices After Harvesting
- Farmer checks mandi prices by:
  - State
  - Crop type
- Helps decide:
  - Where to sell
  - When to sell
- Maximizes profit after harvesting

---

## 👥 8. Community Interaction
- Farmers share experiences and problems
- Ask questions and get suggestions
- Build collaborative learning environment
